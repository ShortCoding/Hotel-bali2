# Hidden Beaches of Uluwatu: Bali's Best Kept Secrets

Uluwatu, known for its dramatic cliffs and world-class surf, harbors some of Bali's most beautiful hidden beaches. Here's your guide to finding these secluded paradises.

## Nyang Nyang Beach

One of Uluwatu's most secluded beaches, accessible via:

- 500-step staircase
- 15-minute walk through greenery
- Limited facilities, bringing supplies recommended
- Perfect for sunset views

## Nunggalan Beach

This hidden gem features:

- Shipwreck photo opportunities
- Crystal clear waters
- Natural caves
- Minimal crowds

## Green Bowl Beach

A small but stunning beach offering:

- Bat caves
- Great snorkeling
- Morning tide pools
- Challenging access keeping crowds away
