# Best Time to Visit Bali: A Complete Guide

Planning the perfect trip to Bali requires understanding its seasonal patterns and what each month has to offer. This guide will help you choose the ideal time for your visit.

## Dry Season (April to October)

The dry season is generally considered the best time to visit Bali. During these months, you can expect:

- Low humidity
- Minimal rainfall
- Perfect beach weather
- Ideal conditions for outdoor activities

### Peak Season (July and August)

While the weather is perfect, these months see:

- Higher prices
- Crowded attractions
- Busy beaches
- Advanced booking required

## Wet Season (November to March)

The wet season has its own charm:

- Lush, green landscapes
- Lower prices
- Fewer tourists
- Brief, heavy showers
