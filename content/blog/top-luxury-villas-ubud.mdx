# Top 10 Luxury Villas in Ubud

Ubud, the cultural heart of Bali, is home to some of the most spectacular private villas in the world. Nestled among rice terraces and lush jungle, these properties offer an unparalleled luxury experience.

## 1. Four Seasons Resort at Sayan

Perched above the Ayung River, the Four Seasons Resort at Sayan offers private villas with infinity pools and stunning valley views. Each villa features:

- Private plunge pool
- Traditional Balinese architecture
- 24-hour butler service
- Outdoor meditation areas

## 2. COMO Shambhala Estate

Known for its wellness focus, COMO Shambhala Estate provides:

- Holistic wellness programs
- Private swimming pools
- Personal assistant service
- Yoga and meditation facilities

## 3. Mandapa, a Ritz-Carlton Reserve

This sanctuary offers:

- River-front villas
- Private gardens
- Traditional Balinese ceremonies
- Personalized dining experiences
